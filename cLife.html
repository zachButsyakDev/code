Despite what some might tell you, the UNO campus is actually pretty alive.

I feel like UNO does a pretty good job of actually preparing young adults for real life, as having to get out there actually takes effort

and will quickly break any expectations that social events will just happen around you without effort.

So while UNO's campus might not exactly be the party school some people might be looking for, there are absolutely things to do.

It just takes some effort. But you can do it, I believe in you :).
