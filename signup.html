How to Enroll:

Enrolling to UNO can be pretty daunting. However, that's why my awesome UNO guide is here for you.

There's a decent list of things you'll need to provide, These include...

  1. Highschool Transcript/GED Score
  2. ACT/SAT Scores
  3. Financial Information
  4. Additional information regarding background
  5. your soul

    6. Compared to most schools, UNO's registration is pretty simple. However, if you need to, schedule a meeting with an advisor to help with the process

    Once you're done, you should receive your email either confirming or denying your enrollment application. Good luck!
