Handy-Dandy Tips to Survive

Just some general information to help get you through your first semester once you're enrolled.

  • GO TO YOUR LECTURES. I promise you, if you're in a tough class, going to every lecture is worth it.
  • Try to get involved in clubs. They can really help make you feel like an actual college student and not just some random guy bumbling through your classes.
  • I don't care who you are, what GPA you had in HS, or what AP classes you took, study. It's like, actually useful.
  • Use the mentors or tutors! They're literally here to help you.
  • Don't try to have a side-project while you're doing your classes. Get your classes done first, and then absolutely hammer down a side project during breaks.
  • Don't stress too much on internships. Wait until you've gotten about halfway through your school. Companies won't choose you to be an intern if you're still in intro to comp-sci.
